Funding Global Health and Scientific Advancement

Desci CellDAO is a decentralized community of researchers, funders, and advocates dedicated to advancing human health, scientific research, and innovation across all disciplines. By harnessing the power of blockchain and decentralized governance, we focus on fostering global collaboration, providing education, and funding transformative research to benefit humanity as a whole.

Phase 1

Pioneering Novel Funding Mechanisms with Blockchain Transparency

  • Leveraging cutting-edge blockchain technology, we create on-chain, transparent funding mechanisms that enable early-stage research and biotech startups to thrive.
  • Our community-driven token structure ensures collaborative capital allocation, aligning interests and incentivizing impactful innovation.
Phase 2

Decentralized Research & Education Network

  • Break down barriers between academia, research and development silos
  • DeMook: Revolutionize global academic knowledge sharing and education
  • DeGit: Build decentralized open-source development network
  • DeSci: Establish cross-institutional scientific research framework
Phase 3

Web3 Innovation Tools

  • Deploy decentralized peer review and research bounty system
  • Launch MetaSci Project Hub for tokenized collaboration and investment
  • Implement decentralized copyright protection and content monetization
  • Create token-powered marketplace for research services
Phase 4

Industry Integration & Ecosystem Maturity

  • Establish partnerships with research institutions and tech companies
  • Launch MetaSci DAO for community governance
  • Create sustainable token economy for research funding
  • Develop industry-standard APIs for research collaboration
